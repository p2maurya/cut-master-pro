# CutMaster Pro - CapCut-Like Video Editor Guide

**Quick Start: Edit Videos Like CapCut!**

---

## 🎯 Getting Started (2 Minutes)

### 1. **Create a New Project**
- From Dashboard, click **"Create New Project"**
- Choose your video aspect ratio (9:16 for TikTok/Reels, 16:9 for YouTube, 1:1 for Square)
- Click **"Create"** and enter editor

### 2. **Upload Your Video**
- Click **M** (Media) button on left toolbar
- Click **"Upload Video"** or drag & drop files
- Select video from "My Videos" library
- Click **"+Add"** to add to timeline

### 3. **Preview & Play**
- Video appears in center preview window
- Use **▶ Play** button to preview
- **Space** key to play/pause (keyboard shortcut)

---

## ✂️ Core Editing (Timeline at Bottom)

### **Adding Clips**
1. Click **M (Media)** panel
2. Browse uploaded videos or stock footage
3. Click "+Add" to add clip to timeline
4. Multiple clips automatically arrange on timeline

### **Trimming Videos**
- **Quick Trim**: Drag clip edges left/right on timeline
- **Precise Trim**: Right-click clip > "Trim Start/End"
- **Split Clip**: Select clip, use **"Split"** button to cut at playhead

### **Deleting Clips**
- Click clip on timeline to select (turns cyan/blue)
- Press **Delete** key or click "Delete" button

### **Rearranging Clips**
- Drag clips left/right on timeline to reorder
- Drop between clips to insert

---

## 🎨 Add Effects & Style

### **Text (T Button)**
1. Click **T (Text)** on left sidebar
2. Type your text in "Write Text" box
3. Choose style: Basic, Title, Caption, or Neon
4. Pick color from color dots (white, pink, blue, yellow, black)
5. Click **"Add Text"** button
6. Text appears on video automatically

### **Effects (FX Button)**
1. Click **FX (Effects)** on left sidebar
2. Choose from: Glitch, Zoom, Shake, Flash, RGB, Film Grain, Neon, etc.
3. Click any effect to apply it instantly

### **Filters & LUTs**
In Effects panel, scroll down to "LUT/Filters":
- **None** - No filter (default)
- **CINE** - Cinematic dark look
- **MOOD** - Moody purple tone
- **WARM** - Warm/sunset tones
- **COOL** - Cool/blue tones
- **VINT** - Vintage retro look
- **B&W** - Black & White
- **NEON** - Neon glow effect

Click any to apply.

### **Transitions (TR Button)**
1. Click **TR (Transitions)** on left sidebar
2. Browse transition types:
   - **Basic**: Cut, Fade, Dissolve
   - **Dynamic**: Zoom, Spin, Slide, Whip Pan, Glitch
3. Click to add between clips

---

## 🎵 Audio & Music

### **Add Music**
1. Click **BG (Background)** button
2. Scroll to "Background Music"
3. Click on music track you like
4. Click **"Add Music"** button
5. Music plays throughout video

### **Upload Your Own Audio**
1. Click **A (Audio)** button
2. Click **"Upload Audio"** button
3. Select your MP3/WAV file
4. Click **"Add Selected"** to add to timeline

---

## ⚙️ Fine-Tune (Right Panel)

### **When a clip is selected:**

**Transform** (Clip Properties):
- **Scale** - Zoom in/out (10-300%)
- **Rotate** - Spin clip (-180 to +180°)
- **Opacity** - Make transparent (0-100%)
- **X/Y Position** - Move left/right, up/down

**Speed**:
- **0.1x** - Super slow motion
- **1x** - Normal speed (default)
- **2x, 4x, 8x, 16x** - Speed up video

**Flip**:
- Horizontal/Vertical flip for mirror effect

**Crop/Aspect**:
- Choose aspect ratio (9:16, 16:9, 1:1, 4:5)
- Select fill or fit mode

**Actions**:
- **Reverse** - Play clip backwards
- **Loop** - Repeat clip

### **Color Adjustments** (Color Tab):
- **Brightness** - Make lighter/darker
- **Contrast** - Increase/decrease contrast
- **Saturation** - Intensify colors
- **Temperature** - Make warmer/cooler

---

## 📤 Export Your Video

### **When Done Editing:**
1. Click **"Export"** button (top-right, pink gradient button)
2. Choose settings:
   - **Resolution**: 480p, 720p, **1080p** (recommended), 4K
   - **Frame Rate**: 24fps, **30fps** (default), 60fps
   - **Format**: **MP4** (best), MOV, GIF
   - **Quality**: Low, **High** (good balance), Best
3. Click **"Export Video Now"**
4. Download starts automatically

## 🎬 Pro Tips (CapCut Style)

### **Fast Editing Workflow**
1. Add all videos to timeline first
2. Then apply transitions & effects
3. Add music last
4. Fine-tune colors and speed
5. Export in high quality

### **Music Sync**
- Add upbeat music first
- Use timeline playhead to cut clips on beat
- Create sync effect with quick cuts

### **Text & Captions**
- Use "Caption" style for subtitles
- Add captions at key moments
- Use different text colors for variety

### **Effects Stacking**
- You can add multiple effects to one clip
- Combine Zoom + Shake for dynamic look
- Use transitions between effects

### **Speed Ramping**
- Split clips to different speeds
- Normal speed → 0.5x → 2x creates drama
- Great for TikTok/Instagram content

### **Color Grading**
- Start with LUT (cinematic, moody, etc.)
- Fine-tune with brightness/contrast
- Warm tone + film grain = retro look

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### **Video won't play**
- Try refreshing (Ctrl+R)
- Check browser console (F12) for errors
- Ensure backend server is running

### **Upload fails**
- Check file size (max 500MB per video)
- Use common formats: MP4, WebM, MOV
- Ensure stable internet connection

### **Export stuck**
- Wait a few minutes (processing)
- Try lower resolution (720p)
- Check browser console for errors

### **Clips not showing**
- Ensure videos uploaded successfully
- Click **"My Videos"** tab in Media panel
- Try refreshing the page
